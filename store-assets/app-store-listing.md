## Description (4000 chars max)

CAST is the ultimate fishing companion app for anglers of all levels. Whether you're after a trophy carp on a UK lake, chasing Golden Dorado in Argentina, or learning to fly fish for Brown Trout — CAST has everything you need.

🐟 AI FISH IDENTIFIER
Point your camera at any fish and instantly identify the species using advanced AI. Get instant species info, rarity rating, legal size requirements, and handling tips.

🗺️ WORLDWIDE FISHING SPOTS
Explore 150+ hand-curated fishing locations across every continent — with species lists, best baits, difficulty ratings, permit requirements, and angler reviews. Search by country, region, or water type.

🎣 CATCH LOG
Record every catch with photos, location, weight, bait used, and conditions. Track your personal bests and build a lifetime fishing journal.

🐠 FISH DATABASE — 130+ SPECIES
Browse our global fish encyclopedia covering species from Common Carp to the mythic Arapaima, Blue Marlin, and Mongolian Taimen. Every fish rated by rarity: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic.

⚡ LIVE CONDITIONS
Real-time tide data, moon phases, solunar feeding windows, UV index, wind and swell forecasts — all in one place so you always know when and where to fish.

🏆 ACHIEVEMENTS & QUESTS
Unlock 40+ achievements as you catch new species, hit personal bests, and fish new locations. Daily and weekly quests keep every session exciting.

🔧 TOOLS
- Rig Builder with 20+ popular rigs
- Knot library with step-by-step guides
- Casting distance calculator
- Bait guide by species and season
- Fishing regulations checker by region
- Moon & solunar calendar

👥 COMMUNITY
Share catches, find clubs, enter competitions, and connect with anglers near you.

CAST is built by anglers, for anglers. No ads on core features. Always improving.
